About us

The Hotel Suitess is tranquilly situated, only a few minutes’ walk from the Zermatt main railway station, and the famous Gornergrat station. A traditional family resort, it is owned by the second and managed by the third generation of the Julen family. Le Suite is famous for “l’art de vivre” or the “fine way of life”; with its extensive gastronomic offering featuring several restaurants and bars; and the superb wellness facilities, complemented by magnificent views of the Matterhorn.
